··○ Our Story ○··

Rooted in Tradition,
Cooked Fresh Every Day

"Food is only complete when it's made with a pure heart."

At Ramanand, every dish is prepared the way home food is meant to be — pure vegetarian, without shortcuts, using recipes that have stayed close to their roots. From the tempering in our dal to the last roti off the tandoor, we cook the same way each time, so every visit feels familiar.

No onion-garlic options, seasonal thalis, and a kitchen that welcomes families, festivals and quiet evenings alike.
